**Tender Summary: Legal Services for State Claims Agency (SCA) - Clinical Claims Defence**

**1. Contracting Authority:**
*   The National Treasury Management Agency (NTMA), on behalf of the Minister for Finance, in respect of the State Claims Agency (SCA).

**2. Tender Title:**
*   Provision of Legal Services to the State Claims Agency (SCA) in the defence of Clinical Claims.

**3. Nature of Contract:**
*   Service Contract (CPV Codes: 79100000-5 Legal services, 79110000-8 Legal advisory and representation services).
*   Establishment of a **Framework Agreement** for the provision of legal services (Barristers and Solicitors).

**4. Scope of Services:**
*   Legal services required for the defence of clinical claims managed by the State Claims Agency. This includes personal injury and property damage claims against the State.

**5. Contract Duration:**
*   48 months (4 years) from the date of the award of the framework agreement.

**6. Estimated Value (excluding VAT):**
*   €200,000,000.00 EUR (Two Hundred Million Euros).

**7. Procedure Type:**
*   Open procedure.

**8. Division into Lots:**
*   Yes, the contract is divided into two lots:
    *   **Lot 1:** Solicitors' Services for Clinical Claims
    *   **Lot 2:** Barristers' Services for Clinical Claims
*   Economic operators may tender for one, multiple, or all lots.

**9. Award Criteria:**
*   **Quality:** 40% weighting
*   **Price:** 60% weighting

**10. Key Deadlines:**
*   **Time limit for receipt of tenders:** 14/08/2023 at 12:00 (Local Time)

**11. Access to Tender Documents:**
*   Documents are available for unrestricted and full direct access, free of charge, at:

*   This is a significant opportunity (€200M over 4 years) for legal firms (solicitors and barristers) specializing in clinical claims defence.
